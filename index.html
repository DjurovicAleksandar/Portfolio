About Me

Who am I?

I am Aleksandar Đurović, a self-taught frontend developer with a year and a half of experience in the field. I have honed my expertise in the front-end field and am constantly seeking opportunities to further develop my abilities.

Working on projects that pose a challenge and require growth is what I love most about this field. I have a strong determination and persistence that drives me to tackle difficult tasks, and I believe that nothing is impossible. My technical skills and attention to detail have allowed me to successfully build websites and applications from scratch, as well as update and maintain existing ones.

In my free time, I enjoy yoga, meditating, and mixing cocktails. I also have a passion for kayaking and the great outdoors, as well as philosophy. I believe in bringing my knowledge from other areas into my work and strive to be a well-rounded individual.

I am open to both freelance and full-time employment opportunities and would love the chance to bring my skills and determination to a new project. If you are interested in working with me, please don't hesitate to reach out!
